Artists can register on MuseApp by creating an account.

Artists must complete their profile with bio, skills, experience, and portfolio.

Portfolio can include images, videos, or audio.

Artists can set pricing as hourly, fixed, or custom.

Artists define a working radius which controls where customers can book them.

Artists manage availability using a calendar.

Artists receive booking requests and can accept or reject them.

Artists get paid after successful booking completion.

Artists can update pricing and availability anytime.
